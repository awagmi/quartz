Background

Wildlife Studios

  • 10 years company based in Brazil
  • 2 billion mobile downloads across apps
  • Top 10 mobile developer

Castle Crush

  • Mobile game with 75mil downloads, and over 1.4 million monthly active users
  • Own and upgrade cards

NFTs in Castle Crush

  • NFTs will be stronger and cooler looking cards
  • Purchase with $AVAX, and also mint using $ACS
  • NFTs mid april, playable in june. phased monthly drop
  • Hope to convert game community into crypto with Avalanche
  • Card Games are a pretty good fit for NFTs
  • Game balancing
    • Game mechanics will be on own server (off chain) initially to react quickly
    • NFT will be used to focus on ownership
    • Once reach reasonable development cycle then might it be moved to the blockchain

Thoughts on transitioning from web2 to web3

@NFT_Silent:

  • Sentiment is similar to web2 f2p games where people were cautious of f2p games with microtransactions
  • Evironmental issues on Avalanche nonexistent
  • Wildlife Studios web2 revenue can be used to fund web3 games
  • No blockchain game has reached critical mass like mobile games yet
    • Their mobile game Sniper3D has close to 500m downloads

@0xJ010:

  • Castle Crush community has not manifested any negative sentiment about push to NFTs yet
  • Most were quite excited about new content to the game
  • Anti-nft sentiment mischaracterized, maybe due to uncertainty in direction

Tackling a web2 audience

  • Most players dont really know how NFTs work
  • Make it as painless and seamless as possible to use $AVAX
    • Automatic wallet creation
    • Gasless Subnet

Why Avalanche?

  1. Support from Ava Labs was great
  2. TPS, time to finality of Avalanche
  3. Road map of Avalanche
  4. Customization of blockchain ( Subnet)

Subnet

Castle Crush Subnet (name not mentioned)

  • gasless to minimize friction
  • marketplace will use $ACS, $AVAX
  • Automatic wallet creation allowed instantaneous earning once they start playing the game

Validators

  • Initial validators will be the team
    • Process of decentralizing it still in process of being decided

Metrics to gauge success

  • Monthly active users
  • Lifetime Value
  • Conversion of current player base to the NFTs
  • Retention - tracking and determining the effect of NFTs in game.

Future Projects

  • 3rd web3 project
